Hello and thank you for demonstrating interest in our program!

 

Our focus for Summer of Side Projects is to provide students an opportunity to learn how to build projects outside of their typical CS courses alongside exposure to different fields of CS through a series of our professional development workshops like resume writing or tech interviewing. We hope by the end of the program you will have a portfolio of projects that you would be able to showcase to your friends, potential employers, and more. But more importantly the ability to work on projects that you are passionate about. 

 

Action Items:

  • Complete the onboarding form: https://forms.gle/sSeXzqE49da3Pvsr9 - 
  • Join the forum and introduce yourself in the Introductions discussion: https://forum.sosp22.com/
  • [optional] If you want to get a head start on learning, we recommend that you download VSCode and Python Libraries before the workshop session: https://sosp22.com/vscode

 

Program Schedule (June 4th - July 2nd):

  • Saturdays (12-1pm CST): we will introduce a new topic for you all to explore. There will also be an optional 1 hour zoom code session where we’ll walk through it as well.
  • Tuesdays (11:59pm CST): turn in a turn in MVP via google form features in the same topic. Don’t overthink it but use this to get going after you’ve gone through the workshop content.
  • Following Friday (11:59pm CST): turn in your final project via google form and make a post on the forum showing what you did to get feedback and encouragement from both fellow students as well as experienced SOSP staff.
  • Sundays (5 - 6pm CST): be on the lookout for our professional development events! We will be having our personal development workshop sessions such as workflow, how to prepare for technical interviews, branding, and more!

 

All the workshops are optional to attend and are recorded, which will be posted on the website after the workshop ends. 

 

We look forward to having a fun summer with you!

Best,
